Research Description

Immersed Finite Element Methods

In scientific research, simulations are used widely among mechanics, physics and engineering application. Multi-domain PDEs with discontinuous coefficients are raised in such situation. I focused on Immersed Finite Element Methods in these interface problems. In this peroid, I aimed on extending conventional IFEM to wider application scenarios including muliti-domain and triple junction points, discontinous jump condition and Partial Penalized IFEM.

Related pyblications:

  • Y.Chen, S.Hou, X.Zhang, Immersed Finite Element Methods for Elliptic Interface Problems with Multi-domain and Triple Junction Points. Adv. Appl. Math. Mech., in press, (2019)

Statistical Applications in Environmental Science

In natural science, conclusoins are obtained from experiment and analysis. Statistical methods have contributed to experimental data analysis. Apart from conventional statistical and probability theory research, I also did some work in applied data analysis in the field of encironmental science equipped with my undergraduate major. In the past 2 years, I participated in data analysis work involoved in water environmental impact assessmemnt of Yangtze River, air pollutant and Nitrogen footprint computation and Taihu Network mechanism energy estimation.
