Research Description

Computational Mathematics Topics

In scientific research, simulations are used widely among mechanics, physics and engineering application. Multi-domain PDEs with discontinuous coefficients are raised in such situation. Immersed Finite Element Methods (Z.Li, 1998) are invented to handle these interface problems. I focused on extending conventional IFEM to wider application scenarios including multi-domain and triple junction points, discontinuous jump condition. Meanwhile, I applied Partial Penalized Immersed Finite Element Method (T.Lin et al, 2015) to modify performance of these extensions. The two diagrams below shows numerical solution of specific 2D elliptic interface problems under condition of multi-domain & triple junction points and discontinuous jump condition respectively.

Environmental Science Topics

In natural science, conclusions are obtained from experiment and analysis. Statistical methods have contributed to experimental data analysis. Apart from conventional statistical and probability theory research, I also did some work in applied data analysis in the field of environmental science equipped with my undergraduate major. In the past 2 years, I participated in data analysis work involved in water environmental impact assessment of Yangtze River, air pollutant and Nitrogen footprint computation and Binhu Network mechanism energy estimation.
